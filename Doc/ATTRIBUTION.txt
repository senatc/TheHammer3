
Origins of the used artwork for TheHammer 3.0

The logo with the Squirrel and the hammer has been drawn by Richard van de Pas specifically for our editor.

tt* icons - DAW Studio Palette (manually extracted and composed into the format required)
Base CE icons - Drawn by Sergey Natarov
30%% of *.ico - DAW distributed for Developers (DF X folder)
others (mostly *.ico) - are from Iconizer, overall 40-50% I think.
